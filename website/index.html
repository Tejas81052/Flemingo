About

I’m Tejas. I’m drawn to projects where the right thing to build is also the harder thing to build. Recent work has focused on Android, browser internals, and the surprisingly large number of UI details that determine whether a piece of software feels good or just looks good.

Projects

Live Android · Kotlin · WebView

Ebors — a privacy-first Android browser

A Chromium-based (via Android System WebView) browser with on-device ad/tracker blocking, true incognito profiles, force-dark, reader mode, find-in-page, and a paper-themed UI. No analytics, no telemetry, no account.

  • ~12,000 lines of Kotlin across 30+ files
  • SQLite-backed history / bookmarks / downloads
  • Signed updateable blocklist with curated EasyList / EasyPrivacy entries
  • Apache-2.0 licensed
